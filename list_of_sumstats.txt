List of files and acronyms
FG: summary statistics for Fasting Glucose GWAS
GLGC: summary statistics for lipids GWAS (LDL and Triglycerides)
ICBP: summary statistics for Blood Pressure (BP) GWAS (Systolic BP and Diastolic BP)
MDD: summary statistics for Major Depressive Disorders
T2D: summary statistics for Type 2 Diabetes
CAD: summary statistics for Coronary Artery Disease
SCZ: summary statistics for Schizophrenia
